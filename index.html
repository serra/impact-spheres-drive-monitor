Impact Spheres Scripts¶

This is a collection of scripts to support our workflow.

Currently, these scripts are used to:

  • Automatically post notifications to our Slack Channel
  • Inspect our Google Drive folders and report flow to Slack
  • Search the Mind Settlers practices database on Contentful
  • Google Docs Add-On for writing guides

Want to contribute? Cool! Ask Marijn on Slack or on Github, he’ll help you to get started.
